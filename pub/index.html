Spreadsheet.js

A modern spreadsheet library.

Designed with for developers.

Star Follow @HuIsJason

Un-opinionated. Vanilla. Customizable. Zero dependencies.

Spreadsheet.js is a modern take on a spreadsheet library, aimed to provide vanilla HTML/JavaScript spreadsheets without any dependencies! With a focus on customizability, spreadsheets are effortlessly configured through objects with a mulitude of options. Users are able to resize their spreadsheets as they please, for a unique experience for every user.


All spreadsheets support user interactions such as adding and deleting rows, clearing the entire spreadsheet, and resizing cells/columns. Cell types also support HTML Input types such as dates, months, time, weeks, along with traditional numbers and text. Give it a try!
